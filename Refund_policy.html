REFUND & RETURN POLICY


Thanks for purchasing our products at mmdt.at operated by Motion Mode Dance Theatre.

We offer a full money back guarantee on all purchases made on our website, but under certain conditions. We invite you to read the conditions explained in more detail below, in order to see the condition that applies to your situation, while following the correct procedure.

Our Returns & Refunds Policy allow you to return an article purchased anytime

If you have any further questions or would like to request a refund, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Refund will not be made:
- in case of your illness

Returns can be made:
- if the country in which you live or the country in which the event is planned makes it difficult to leave the country.
- in the event of martial law in the country in which you live or the country where the event is planned.

Each case will be considered separately

Refunds (if applicable)

Once your returned article is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned article. We will also inform you if your refund has been approved or refused.

If your request is approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original payment method anytime we can do this. If you need to pay a bank commission when returning, this will be done on our part.

If you haven't received a refund yet, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company, it may take some time before your refund is officially posted.

Next contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted. If you've done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at
