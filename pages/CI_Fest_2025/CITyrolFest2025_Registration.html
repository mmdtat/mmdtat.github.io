Helper Positions Open – Join the Festival Team!

We’re opening 12 Helper positions for the festival!

Helpers receive a reduced participation fee in exchange for their support with essential tasks. This is a great way to contribute to the community and be part of the festival in a special way.

What’s involved:
– Arriving earlier and staying for the entire festival
– Helping fix and transport the dance floor before and after the festival (transportation is provided)
– Keeping the space clean and organized
– Taking care of dishes before and after meals
– Managing the first aid kit and trash
– Supporting the festival schedule
– Assisting with other important tasks as needed

If you're interested in being a Helper, please register directly – spots are limited!
