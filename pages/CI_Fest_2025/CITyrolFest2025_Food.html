Accommodation and Food




Accommodation


The festival will take place in the beautiful city of Innsbruck, and we can't wait to welcome you! If you've secured your early bird ticket, we highly recommend booking your accommodation as soon as possible through trusted platforms like Airbnb or Booking.com.

In previous years, we tried to arrange accommodations through friends of friends, but this year, our team is actively exploring new solutions to make your stay even

smoother. If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to our organizing team at soloandcityrolfest@gmail.com
We're here to help!

See you in Innsbruck!

Food


We’re excited to share that our friends will be organizing catering for the festival, offering delicious vegetarian Oriental meals. The catering will include two meals per day for 8 days, at an estimated cost of €160-260.

Food registration will open in June/July, and we’ll notify all participants via email when it’s time to sign up.

We’re confident you’ll love the vibrant flavors and care that goes into each meal. Stay tuned for more updates!
