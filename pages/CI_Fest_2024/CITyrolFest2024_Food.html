Accommodation


Dear Festival Attendees,

We wanted to take a moment to address queries regarding accommodation and clarify our recommendation to stay in and around Innsbruck.

By recommending lodgings in close proximity to Innsbruck, we aim to ensure that your festival experience is not only artistically enriching but also logistically smooth. This allows you to immerse yourself fully in the city's cultural tapestry without the hassle of extended commutes.

We appreciate your understanding and flexibility in this matter, and we are confident that your stay in and around Innsbruck will add to the overall enjoyment of the festival. Should you have any specific questions or require assistance with accommodation arrangements, please feel free to reach out to our dedicated support team.

We look forward to welcoming you to the vibrant city of Innsbruck and sharing the magic of dance with you at our festival.
