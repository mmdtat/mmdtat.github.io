Accommodation


We present two distinct accommodation options, each designed to elevate your stay in Innsbruck and add a touch of luxury to your festival adventure.

Option 1: Montagu (from 33 euro/night)
In our stylishly renovated sleeping cabins, you will have your own little space with a reading lamp, a socket, and a locker for your luggage and valuables. There is enough space to chill in your room or if you feel like you are welcome to join the other travelers in cooking, relaxing, or having a chat in the common area on the third floor.

  • Private double room with shared bathroom
  • 6 bed cabin dorm rooftop
  • 6 bed mixed


Option 2: Pension Paola (from 46 euros/night)
Our bed & breakfast features single, double, triple, and quad rooms, most of which have ensuite shower and WC. All room rates are per room per night including breakfast.
