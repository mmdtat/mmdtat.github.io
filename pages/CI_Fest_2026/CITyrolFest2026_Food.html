Accommodation and Food


Accommodation

This is a non-residential event, but we support arranging accommodation.


The festival will take place in the beautiful city of Innsbruck, and we can't wait to welcome you! If you've secured your early bird ticket, we highly recommend booking your accommodation as soon as possible through trusted platforms like Airbnb or Booking.com.

In previous years, we tried to arrange accommodations through friends of friends, but this year, our team is actively exploring new solutions to make your stay even smoother.

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to our organizing team at soloandcityrolfest@gmail.com
We're here to help!

See you in Innsbruck!




Festival Food


We have wonderful news!

This year, our kitchen will be in the hands of someone truly special — a cook who is also a dancer and brings the same care and creativity to food as we do to movement. We are so happy to have her with us.

As we are organising a dedicated kitchen space to make mealtimes comfortable and communal for everyone, we kindly ask you to register for meals as early as possible.



Maya Schwartz


A dancer, vegan cook, choreographer, and teacher of release technique, contact improvisation, and yoga, as well as an Ilan Lev therapist.
Originally from Ukraine and Israel–Palestine, she has worked with choreographers such as Roni Chadash, Gil Kerer, Maya Brinner, Michal Ben Lior, Sally Ann Friedland, Lilach Pnina Livne, and Judi Adini, and collaborated with Vertigo Dance Company on The Birth of the Phoenix and Vertigo POB.

Her choreographic works include Primal Feelings (duet), Alata (duet), and solo pieces such as Nothing and Growing Pains. Maya has performed and taught at institutions and festivals worldwide, including Masdanza (Gran Canaria), Zürcher Theater Spektakel, Bratislava in Movement, Dance Days Chania (Greece), and Prisma Festival (Panama).

She also co-organized workshops and events through Deep Into You, a series of retreats blending art and movement in nature across Israel–Palestine, Sinai, Portugal, France, and Peru.

Worked as a cook at Fall Jam Freiburg, Sweden CI festival, Larret en moments, Larretude Program, BodyWeather training and more!
