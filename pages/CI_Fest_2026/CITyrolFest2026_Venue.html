Venue


The festival's dancing venue for 2025 will be the well-known Künstlerhaus Büchsenhausen, a space familiar to our participants from previous years. Nestled in the heart of Innsbruck, the venue offers an inspiring environment for creativity and performance. It is renowned for its connection to contemporary art and its commitment to fostering artistic collaboration.
