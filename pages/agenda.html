Here you go... There is no way to be bored this year, because the organization team of our festival has prepared the schedule full of the events.

*Please, pay attention to the registration time. If you arrive later on, tell us in advance.

*The food will be provided by us 15 times, if you book it in registration form. Pay attention that in the day off everyone takes responsibility for the food by themselves (you can oder food at the owners of the space for 5 euro-breakfast, 7 - lunch and 7-dinner; or you can buy food at the shop).

*Morning outdoor workshops of Tamara and Angie depend on the climate we have. In case of bad weather, we wake up in dancing space.

*On Friday, 14th in the morning is a possibility to share some workshops or private lesson from participant to participants. That means if you have any skill or experience to share and you feel like that, please, during the first 3 days of the Festival we can add it in the timetable.

*In the Day off we have an interesting program of hicking to the summits, swimming in the lake or swimming pool, observing the nature around, and taking a real rest.
