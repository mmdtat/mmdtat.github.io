Contact Improvisation Tyrol Festival 2019 this year changed location from Fernsteinsee close to Innsbruck. All participants, teachers, videomakers, musicians will live together in a suburb of lovely Innsbruck Land in organized Kranerbitter Camping surrounded by mountains. Every morning training of Gaga will be in the lawn close to River "Inn" and mountains with live music. In the second half of the day, all festival will move to the city center of old tiny Innsbruck. Rest of Workshops will be in Street Motion Studio.
We found these changes more attractive for this year. The first you can combine nature and small-city life what brings you being more involved in local particular Tyrolian life. We will have a beautiful place to eat and to dance.
All changes lead us in a better way always...
