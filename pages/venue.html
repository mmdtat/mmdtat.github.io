Accommodation and food


We've found a nice guest house for the festival in 15 minutes by foot from the dancing space. Breakfast and lunch will be served over there. The interior designed in a traditional Tyrolean style, which would help to dive into the atmosphere of old times of these lands and get in touch with local culture. There you have an opportunity to stay in a 6-shared beds room (20 euros per day) or if you seek for a little bit more privacy, double rooms with double bets are also at your disposal (25 euros per day).
Check out the place - www.lacknerhof-tirol.at

Dancing space


This year we have a special dancing space with a long dancing history. In medieval times, it was a ballroom. Time was passing but the space has been always belonged to the dance culture and filled with a dance spirit fully. Nowadays, it's a ballet school with weekly Contact classes and Contemporary workshops. It is located in the center of the of St. Johann downtown.
