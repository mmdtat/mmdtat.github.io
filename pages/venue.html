Dear participants, we moved to cozy and charming Obendorf (close to St. Johann in Tirol). That tiny village, surrounded by high peaks of Wilder Kaiser mountain ridge, will pull us into its arms in a warm and tight hugs.


How to get there?


If you plan to take a flight, we would recommend to check on Innsbruck, Vienna or Munich, these are the most convenient destinations, and the transfer to Obendorf will not take much time. You can check railway schedule or Bus connections by the following links:

https://www.bahn.com - German Railways
https://www.oebb.at – Austrian railways
https://global.flixbus.com/ - Economy Bus

Dancing and living spaces


This year we are going to have a special venue with a long history. The building belongs to medieval times. Interior designed in a traditional Tyrolean style, which would help to dive into the atmosphere of old times of these lands and get in touch with local culture. Nowadays, it's a gest house with big sport room, accommodation for 100 people, kitchen, bar, dining room and a huge area around, where we can dance, rest, make shooting or music jams.
Breakfast and lunch will be served over there.
You will have an opportunity to stay in a 6-shared beds room (20 euros per day) or if you seek for a little bit more privacy, double rooms with double beds are also at your disposal (25 euros per day).
