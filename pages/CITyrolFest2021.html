August, 9th-15th, 2021

We are happy to announce our third annual "Solo & CI Tirol Festival 2021" which will take place in Austria this year. We believe that our mission is to maintain and strengthen physical and mental health that becomes vital as never before.

The "Solo & CI Festival" is a safe space for you, your friends and your family, where you will have a chance to get acquainted with contemporary dance techniques, contact tango, physical theater, classical contact improvisation and take part in daily jams. Being surrounded with spectacular Tyrolean landscapes, its impressive mountain ridges, virgin forests, and pure untouchable nature you will get a perfect chance to recharge your batteries and find new meanings and directions.

This year we have expanded our program and invited new world-famous teachers.

There is going to be an additional opportunity to participate in a closed group of physical theatre which will work on a mini-performance throughout the festival and present it on the last day. In case you are interested, please read the project description carefully and indicate your participation in this course during the registration.

"Solo & CI Tirol Festival" is thrilled to invite participants from 11 to 90 years old.

Please don't hesitate to contact us in case of any questions.

Dancing and living spaces


We have a special venue with a long history. The building belongs to medieval times. Interior designed in a traditional Tyrolean style, which would help to dive into the atmosphere of old times of these lands and get in touch with local culture. Nowadays, it's a gest house with big sport room, accommodation for 100 people, kitchen, bar, dining room and a huge area around, where we can dance, rest, make shooting or music jams.
Breakfast and lunch will be served over there.
You will have an opportunity to stay in a 6-shared beds room (22,2 euros per day) or if you seek for a little bit more privacy, double rooms with double beds are also at your disposal (32,2 euros per day).
There is also possibility to stay in your own car (5 euros per day).
