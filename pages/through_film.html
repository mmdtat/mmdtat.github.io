Tamara Maksymenko and Lukasz Czapski are delighted to present the joint project: dance film THROUGH.

Where professional and non-professional artists worked together on creating the film during 5 days of intense workshops in Innsbruck city.

The concept of the project “THROUGH” is “Here and Now” and very spontaneous, workshop participants had improvised with a clear score during the piece.

We dove deeper into our daily life’s obstacles and how we, humans, struggle THROUGH them. We asked ourselves questions, like: What is the impact of our choice? Uncovering lies and truth is a big part of this workshop. Humans blurr and smash their own truth, playing the game of “who am I?” every single day. In the project “THROUGH”, we had discussed and danced about things that touch and botherus.

Music: Jochen Hampl Video by: Vitali Sviridenko and Aleksey Vidishev Artists: Doris Hinsteiner, Rebecca Plattner, Jochen Hampl, Nicolaj Konstantin Astl, Anja Eder, Tamara Maksymenko, Juliana Haider, Jade Manrique Tello, Lukasz Czapski.
