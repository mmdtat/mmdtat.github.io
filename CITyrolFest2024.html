Welcome to the Contact Improvisation Festival in Tirol, where the art of movement converges with the breathtaking beauty of the Tirolean nature. Immerse yourself in an extraordinary celebration of dance, surrounded by the majestic Alps and lush landscapes.

Our festival is more than just a gathering; it's a testament to high-quality artistic expression. Explore a rich tapestry of worykshops, and collaborative experiences curated to elevate your understanding of movement and connection. Delve into the essence of contact improvisation, guided by experienced instructors, and discover the transformative power of embodied expression.

We recognize the importance of preserving and honoring historical and political significance of Contact Improvisation. Through dance and lectures we weave narratives that echo the stories of the CI, fostering a connection between past, present, and future.

Join us in this unique fusion of nature, art, and history. The Contact Improvisation Festival in Tirol awaits, promising a harmonious blend of creativity and cultural exploration.
