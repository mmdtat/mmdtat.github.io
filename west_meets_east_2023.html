We are thrilled to invite you to our second dance festival in Turkey. This time we are going to make it close to Antalya in the mountains region - Geyikbayırı. It's facianating location with entient spots around, mountains and sea - 25 min by car.

This year we have two teachers and two different and helpful disciplene: Axys Syllabus by... and Contact Improvisation by Tamara Maksymenko

Place

Shortly about the space. We have found an amazing campsite in the mountains in one of the most popular climbing areas in the World - Geyikbayiri. There is cozy accommodation, as well as a 102 m2 dance room with a view of 360 degrees, and meals 2 times per day (Breakfast and Dinner).
The closest airport - is Antalya; which makes your travel to the campsite easier. From Antalya airport - you can take a taxi which will cost approximately 20 euros to the spot, or public transport which is approximately about 3 euros.
The festival can help participants to organize transport from Airport to Geyikbayiri.
